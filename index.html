Ahmed Khaled

akregeb@gmail.com

I’m a B.Sc. student at Cairo University expecting graduation in July 2020.

My research interests are in stochastic and distributed optimization and federated learning. My CV is here.

I was fortunate to intern in the group of Peter Richtárik at KAUST in the summer of 2019 where I worked on the theory of distributed & stochastic optimization. Prior to this, I did some (applied) research on accelerating the training of neural networks under the supervision of Professor Amir Atiya.

Distributed Fixed Point Methods with Compressed Iterates with Sélim Chraibi, Dmitry Kovalev, Peter Richtárik, Adil Salim, and Martin Takáč.

Tighter Theory for Local SGD on Identical and Heterogeneous Data with Konstantin Mishschenko and Peter Richtárik. Accepted to Artificial Intelligence and Statistics (AISTATS) 2020. Extended version of the workshop papers 1 and 2.

Better Communication Complexity for Local SGD with Konstantin Mishschenko and Peter Richtárik. Oral presentation at the NeurIPS 2019 Federated Learning Workshop.
