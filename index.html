Ahmed Khaled

akregeb at gmail dot com

I’m a final-year undergraduate student at Cairo University. My research interests are in optimization.

I was fortunate to intern in the group of Prof. Peter Richtárik at KAUST in the summer of 2019 where I worked on the distributed & stochastic optimization. Prior to this, I did some (applied) research on accelerating the training of neural networks with Prof. Amir Atiya.
